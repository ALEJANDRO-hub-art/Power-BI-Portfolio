# HR Analysis

## Project Overview

### Dataset Description
The HR Analytics dataset contains information on cemployees' details, including their EmpID, Age, AgeGroup, Attrition, BusinessTravel, 
DailyRate, Department, DistanceFromHome, JobID, Education, EnvironmentSatisfaction, Gender, Hourly Rate, Job Role, JobSatisfaction, 
MaritalStatus, MonthlyIncome, MonthlyRate, TotalWorkingYears, YearsAtCompany, and other employee details. The dataset is focused 
on the employee profile attributes.

### Objectives (Business challenge):
- Analyze the gender distribution of employees and identify their exact numbers.
- Analyze the account balances of male and female employees to assess whether any gender-based disparities exist in attrition balances.
- Analyze attition by year in the company. 
- Identify the attrition by education.
- Identify the attrition by salary indicating the age range of the different employees.
- Identify the attrition by salary showing the total count corresponding to each salaryslab
- Identify the attrition by by job role and display the total account for each of them.

### Data Preparation (Data Cleaning in Power Query):
- Remove duplicates from the dataset.
- Data standardization.
- Data normalization.
- Remove BLANK rows from the dataset.
- Changed data types for each column.

### Data View (Data Model)
- Displayed employee count, attrition, attrition rate, avg age, avg salary and avg years in the company with cards.
- Added a Slicer to filter the departments.
- Displayed attrition by gender showing count with a Treemap.
- Displayed attrition by education and their count in a donut chart.
- Displayed attrition by salary with the age group range and their respective count with stacked column chart.
- Showed the job satisfaction rate by job role and count using a matrix.
- Displayed attrition by salary with salary slab and the count in a stacked bar chart.
- Visualized attrition by years at the company with its corresponding count in a area chart. 
- Displayed attrition by job role and its corresponding count in a stacked bar chart.
- Added the analysis title of the HR to the report using a text box.
